Throughout my internship at Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited, as part of the FCAJ Workforce program, from May 5, 2026 to July 30, 2026, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply the knowledge I acquired at university to a real-world project. I participated in designing and presenting an architectural model for building a live service on AWS following a GitOps approach, serving a multiplayer online game. The processing flow runs from CloudFront/WAF at the edge layer, through API Gateway and Lambda for business logic, to DynamoDB for storing player state, along with authentication via Cognito and safe deployment using CodeDeploy in canary release mode. Through this experience, I improved my skills in system architecture design, writing Infrastructure as Code, building automated CI/CD pipelines, as well as the ability to present and explain a complex technical architecture in a clear and coherent manner.  
In terms of work ethic, I always strived to complete assigned tasks well, complied with workplace regulations, and actively communicated with my mentor as well as other interns to improve work efficiency.
